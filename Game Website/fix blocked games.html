Are the games blocked?

Here's how to fix them.

Most schools/workplaces will have a network firewall in place to prevent students/workers from slacking. Thankfully we have a way to jump that firewall. Startpage.com is a website that makes jumping the wall easy. You can go there to access most websites easily with a tool they call "anonymous view". To use anonymous view just search for the website you want to access then press the anonymous view button.
